Dispatching of supply and exhaust ventilation

the functions we perform:

Visual display of the technological process

We provide a user-friendly interface for visually tracking and analysing various aspects of the process. Customers can monitor parameters, equipment status and efficiency in real time.

Prompt change of process parameters, operating modes, equipment

We provide the ability to quickly change system parameters and operating modes depending on changing production conditions or other factors. This allows you to optimise the system to achieve the best possible performance.

Remote monitoring and control of system operation

Our systems provide remote control, monitoring and management of engineering systems from any location. This allows you to quickly respond to changes and adjust parameters through our platforms.

Prompt notification of emergency conditions and exceeding the limits of the technological operating schedule

Our systems instantly alert you to any emergency or deviation from normal operation. This allows us to respond quickly to problems and ensure uninterrupted operations

Work on schedule

We ensure that the systems schedules are followed precisely, which helps to plan production processes and optimise resource use

Maintaining a work history log with event recording

We guarantee precise adherence to the systems operation schedule, which allows us to plan production processes and optimise resource use.

Errors we detect and failures we prevent

Failure of the heat exchanger to freeze / overheating of the electric heater

Our systems react instantly to any temperature deviations that could lead to a heat exchanger freezing or an electric heater overheating. We automatically shut down or adjust the equipment to avoid emergency situations.

Supply/exhaust fan pressure drop alarm condition

Our systems monitor the operation of the fans and detect any pressure deviations. If an alarm condition is detected, we automatically shut down the fans and send a notification for further diagnosis and correction.

Supply/exhaust filter clogging failure

Our systems monitor the condition of utility system filters and detect any blockages. If a problem is detected, we send a notification and can automatically start the filter cleaning or replacement process.

Warning about going out of the control range

Our systems reliably monitor the parameters of engineering systems and detect any deviations from the set values. If an out-of-range condition is detected, we immediately notify you and automatically take the necessary measures to stabilise the situation.
